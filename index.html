About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer at Brain Station 23 PLC specializing in mobile application development. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET).

My professional experience spans over 4 years in software development, working on various mobile applications using Flutter framework. I have contributed to international projects and developed apps for major companies including British American Tobacco Bangladesh. My work has been published in IEEE conferences, focusing on machine learning applications in healthcare and cybersecurity.

I am passionate about Machine Learning, Deep Learning, HCI, Large Language Models, Cybersecurity, NLP, and Neuromorphic Computing. I actively pursue research opportunities and have achieved a GRE score of 303 and IELTS score of 7.5, preparing for advanced studies in these areas.

Research

My research interests span across Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and emerging technologies

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Specializing in neural network architectures, transfer learning, and computer vision applications, particularly in medical image analysis and Alzheimer's Disease classification from MRI images.

Cybersecurity & NLP

Focused on prompt injection detection using ensemble methods combining BERT and LSTM with GloVe embeddings, enhancing security in natural language processing systems.

Human-Computer Interaction

Exploring trust, job security, and collaboration dynamics in automated workplaces transitioning to GenAI, contributing to the understanding of human factors in AI adoption.
